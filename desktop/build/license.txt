--- PART II: PRIVACY POLICY ---

This Privacy Policy explains how KenyaBooks ("we", "us", or "our") handles your information when you use the KenyaBooks Desktop Application ("Software").

1. OUR OFFLINE-FIRST PHILOSOPHY
We built KenyaBooks with privacy and data ownership as foundational principles. The Software operates entirely on your local machine. We do not require you to create an account with us, nor do we require a persistent internet connection to function (except when accessing specific third-party portals or updates).

2. DATA WE DO NOT COLLECT
Because the Software is offline-first:
- We DO NOT collect, store, or transmit your financial data, invoices, client information, inventory records, or employee details to our servers.
- We DO NOT collect your KRA PIN, eTIMS credentials, NHIF, or NSSF login information.
- We DO NOT run remote telemetry or tracking scripts on your local database.
All such data is stored exclusively in a local SQLite database residing on your computer's hard drive.

3. DATA WE MAY COLLECT
While your financial data remains local, the Software may collect minimal information solely for the purpose of maintaining and improving the Software:
(a) Update Checks: The Software may periodically check our servers to determine if a newer version is available. During this process, we may log your IP address, operating system version, and the current version of the Software you are running.
(b) Crash Reports (Opt-In): If the Software crashes, you may be prompted to send a crash report. This report may include technical details such as memory state, stack traces, and system configuration. It is intended to contain no personal financial data, but may inadvertently contain file paths or application state data. You have the right to review and decline sending crash reports.

4. THIRD-PARTY PORTALS
The Software contains embedded web browsers (webviews) that allow you to access government and banking portals (e.g., KRA iTax, eTIMS). 
When you interact with these portals:
- You are communicating directly with the servers of those respective third parties.
- The Company does not intercept, read, or store the traffic between you and these third-party services.
- Your data privacy and security during these sessions are governed by the privacy policies of those third-party organizations (e.g., the Government of Kenya).

5. DATA SECURITY
Although we do not hold your data, we strongly encourage you to implement robust security practices on your local machine:
- Use strong passwords or biometric authentication for your operating system user account.
- Enable full-disk encryption (e.g., BitLocker, FileVault).
- Implement a reliable, encrypted backup strategy for your computer.
We are not responsible for unauthorized access to the device on which you run the Software.

6. YOUR RIGHTS
Because we do not hold your personal financial data, any requests for data access, correction, deletion, or export must be fulfilled by you directly within the Software interface or by querying the local SQLite database.
